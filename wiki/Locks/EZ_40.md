EZ_40 is one of the very first locks you'll run into in hackmud.

# Solutions

EZ_40 expects one of the unlock commands and an ez_prime.

## Unlock commands
open
release
unlock

## ez_prime

The ez_prime is a prime number above 0 and below 100.
