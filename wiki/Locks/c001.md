c001 is one of the very first locks you'll run into in hackmud.

# Solutions

c001 expects one of 8 colors and a color_digit.

## Colors
green
lime
blue
cyan
purple
red
orange
yellow

## Color digit

The color_digit is the length of the color you are attempting.

4
5
6

# Example unlock

eg user.entry_abc123{c001:"red"}
